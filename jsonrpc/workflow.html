sequenceDiagram User->>+Exchange: filecoin_address Exchange-->>+Service: sign_transaction(tx, prvkey) Service-->>-Exchange: signed_message Exchange-->>+Service: send_signed_tx(signed_message) Service-->>-Node: broadcast signed transaction Node-->>+Service: cid_message Service-->>+Exchange: cid_message Exchange->>User: cid_message
